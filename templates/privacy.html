Data we Collect.

Personal data, or personal information ("Personal Data"), means any information about an individual from which that person can be identified. It does not include data where the identity has been removed (anonymous data).

By using our Sites or Services, you are authorising us to gather, parse, and retain data related to the provision of the Service. We collect, use, store, and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together as follows based on the services you use:

Identity Data includes your first name, last name, user role (tenant, flatmate, or owner), gender, and chosen profile avatar.

Contact Data includes your email address, 10-digit mobile number, and your preferred city of residence (e.g., Shimla, Dharamshala, Manali).

Verification Data includes government-issued identification documents, specifically Aadhaar card front and back images, collected securely when you submit a Tenant Verification Request.

Rental & Property Data includes specific property addresses, PIN codes, rent amounts, deposit amounts, landlord names, tenant names, and furniture inventories collected when you use our Rental Agreement or Rent Receipt generators.

Technical & Usage Data includes device internet protocol (IP) address, browser type and version, time zone setting, and information about how you navigate and use our Sites or Services.

Security Data includes the temporary 6-digit Email OTPs (One Time Passwords) generated to authenticate your secure login sessions.

We do not knowingly collect any Special Categories of Personal Data (this includes details about your race or ethnicity, religious or philosophical beliefs, sex life, sexual orientation, political opinions, trade union membership, or genetic and biometric data).
