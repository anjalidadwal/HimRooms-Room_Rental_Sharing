HimRooms Refund and Cancellation Policy

Welcome to HimRooms, Himachal Pradesh's trusted platform for finding the perfect room, PG, or flatmate. In order to contact other users directly or access our Premium Properties, you must be a premium user. We want to ensure that your experience with us is transparent and enjoyable, so we’ve outlined our Refund and Cancellation Policy for our two distinct plans: the DIY Plan and the RM Plan.

Subscription Plans

DIY (Do It Yourself) Plan:

Here’s what you need to know about our DIY Plan:

  • Upon purchasing our 30-day Premium Plan, you will immediately gain access to contact other users directly and view detailed premium listings across cities like Shimla, Dharamshala, and Manali.
  • If, within the first month of your subscription, you are unable to find a suitable accommodation or flatmate, you may request a refund.
  • You will be eligible for the refund once your premium period ends. That means you can request a refund only after a month from the purchase date.
  • Refund Breakdown: Your payment consists of the "Value of Service" and "Platform Fees/Applicable Taxes." Only the "Value of Service" portion is eligible for a refund.

RM (Relationship Manager) Plan:

Our RM Plan offers a personalized approach, combining the benefits of the DIY Plan with additional dedicated support:

  • Upon purchasing the RM Plan, a dedicated Relationship Manager will be assigned to you. They will actively contact potential flatmates or property owners on your behalf and coordinate property visits.
  • You can expect a call from your Relationship Manager within 24 hours of payment confirmation. Support will be provided exclusively over the phone.
  • Your Relationship Manager will be available to assist you on working days from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM, excluding Sundays and Public Holidays.
  • If you wish to request a refund, you can do so after 30 days of payment. You will be eligible for a 50% refund of the amount paid, unless your Relationship Manager has already arranged 10 visits for you, in which case a refund will not be granted.
  • Refunds are not applicable if you have failed to engage with your Relationship Manager after their attempts to contact you for several consecutive days (including unanswered calls and messages).
  • Failure to respond to Relationship Manager calls for several days after visiting a property or meeting a flatmate arranged by them renders you ineligible for a refund.

Identity & Tenant Verification

  • Our tenant verification process relies on Aadhaar identity verification and background checks. Once you submit your Aadhaar documents through our Verification portal, the process begins immediately.
  • All personal data is handled securely and used solely for verification purposes. While we strive to ensure accuracy and security, we are not liable for any disputes or incidents arising after verification.
  • Fees paid specifically for Tenant Verification add-ons are strictly non-refundable once the background check process has been initiated with our third-party partners.

Additional Considerations

  • If you choose to extend your Premium Membership for an additional month, you will not be eligible for a refund as it is compensated with a free extension.
  • If you disagree with or do not fully accept the terms of this refund policy, we kindly ask that you refrain from purchasing our 30-day plan, as we do not entertain immediate cancellation requests.
  • Refunds will not be granted if you seek a flatmate of a different gender, as the success rate in such cases is extremely low.
  • If you received a discount on your plan purchase, the discounted amount will be adjusted within your refund.
  • You are eligible for a refund only if you request it within 3 months of your original purchase date.
  • Property/Flat Owners are eligible for a maximum 50% refund on the DIY Plan. This refund policy is not applicable to accounts registered as PG Owners.
  • Eligibility for a refund is limited to two instances per user account. Once you have been refunded twice, further refund requests will not be considered.
  • If you have made multiple purchases of our premium membership plan, only the most recent payment is eligible for a refund assessment.
  • When you delete your profile, we promptly remove all of your data from our databases. Because of this data deletion, we are unable to process or facilitate refunds for deleted accounts.
  • Approved refunds will be processed within 5 to 7 business days after we receive the required banking details from the customer.
Important Fraud Warning: HimRooms cannot offer refunds for any payments made directly to another user (such as token money or booking advances for a flat). We highly recommend exercising extreme caution and only providing advance payments after personally visiting the property and verifying the owner's credentials.

Prior to making a purchase, please ensure you have read and accepted this refund policy. Thank you for choosing HimRooms!
